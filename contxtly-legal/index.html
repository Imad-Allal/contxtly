Read. Highlight. Learn.

A Chrome extension that translates any word in context, shows you the grammar, and saves it to your vocabulary list.

Get Contxtly

What it does

  • Highlight a word on any webpage — get an instant, context-aware translation.
  • See a grammar breakdown of the full sentence it came from.
  • Save words to a personal vocabulary list, synced across devices.
  • Export to Anki when you're ready to review.

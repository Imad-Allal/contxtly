Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 4, 2025

1. Overview

Contxtly ("we", "us", "our") is a browser extension that helps you learn German by translating words in context. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

2. Data We Collect

When you use Contxtly, we collect:

  • Account information — your name and email address, obtained via Google Sign-In through Supabase.
  • Saved words — German words and phrases you choose to save, along with their translations and the URL of the page where you saved them.
  • Usage data — the number of translations you perform per day, used to enforce plan limits.
  • Payment information — processed securely by Stripe. We never store your card details.

We do not collect your browsing history, the full text of pages you visit, or any data beyond what is necessary to provide the translation service.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To authenticate you and maintain your account.
  • To translate selected German text using our AI-powered pipeline.
  • To sync your saved words across devices.
  • To enforce daily translation limits based on your plan.
  • To process subscription payments via Stripe.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — authentication and database. Privacy Policy
  • Groq — AI inference for translations. Text you select to translate is sent to Groq. Privacy Policy
  • Stripe — payment processing. Privacy Policy

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in Supabase (PostgreSQL) with row-level security policies that ensure you can only access your own data. All data is transmitted over HTTPS.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

You can delete your saved words at any time from within the extension. To delete your account and all associated data, contact us at support@contxtly.com. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

Contxtly is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date above.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this policy, email us at support@contxtly.com.
